What makes Homeverse different from other platforms? expand_more
Homeverse leverages high-tech glassmorphism UI and advanced matching algorithms to connect high-intent buyers with luxury properties. We prioritize transparency, verified listings, and a seamless digital transaction experience that traditional platforms lack.
Are there any membership fees for buyers? expand_more
Browsing and contacting agents on Homeverse is completely free for buyers. We only charge small service fees during successful closing transactions and for premium listing features for sellers.
How do I schedule a property tour? expand_more
Simply click the "Book a Tour" button on any listing page. You can choose between an in-person viewing or a high-definition 3D virtual tour guided by one of our verified partner agents.

domain Listing Your Property

How long does it take for a listing to go live? expand_more
To ensure the quality and safety of our marketplace, every listing undergoes a verification process. This typically takes 12 to 24 hours. Once approved, your property will be visible to thousands of potential buyers instantly.
Can I edit my listing after it is published? expand_more
Yes, you can edit prices, descriptions, and photos at any time via your Dashboard. Major changes may trigger a brief re-verification to maintain site integrity.
